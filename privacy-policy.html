Overview

This is a demo privacy policy page for development and testing of the SMM Manager app. This page explains what personal data (if any) the application collects and how it is used.

Data collected

For testing purposes, this app does not collect or store personal data on the public demo. When deployed, the application may store user-supplied account tokens to integrate with social platforms. Tokens are kept securely and are not shared with third parties.

Use of data

Data is used only to publish content to connected social accounts and to show publishing status. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
